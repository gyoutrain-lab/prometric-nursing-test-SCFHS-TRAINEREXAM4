Question 3

A post‑operative patient suddenly reports shortness of breath. Oxygen saturation is 89%.

  • A. Document complaint and reassess in 15 minutes
  • B. Encourage deep breathing exercises
  • C. Apply oxygen and assess airway and breathing
  • D. Notify physician before intervening

Correct Answer: C

Rationale: Immediate oxygenation and ABC assessment is within nursing scope and addresses life‑threatening hypoxia.

Why others are incorrect:

  • A — Delay error. Documentation without action.
  • B — Inadequate response to hypoxia.
  • D — Scope delay. Action is allowed before MD contact.

Question 4

A patient receiving IV fluids complains of chest tightness. Which response follows Prometric logic?

  • A. Stop infusion and assess airway and circulation
  • B. Slow the infusion and observe
  • C. Call the physician immediately
  • D. Reassure the patient

Correct Answer: A

Rationale: Potential fluid overload or reaction requires immediate stop and ABC assessment.

Why others are incorrect:

  • B — Partial action without safety assessment.
  • C — Delays nursing intervention.
  • D — Unsafe reassurance.

Question 5

A confused patient tries to get out of bed without assistance.

  • A. Apply restraints immediately
  • B. Call family to calm patient
  • C. Ensure safety, stay with patient, and call for help
  • D. Document behavior

Correct Answer: C

Rationale: Fall prevention through immediate supervision and safety action is required.

Why others are incorrect:

  • A — Excessive and scope‑restricted.
  • B — Delays safety.
  • D — Documentation without intervention.

Question 6

A nurse notices IV infiltration at the insertion site.

  • A. Document and continue infusion
  • B. Stop infusion and assess site
  • C. Notify physician immediately
  • D. Reassess after shift change

Correct Answer: B

Rationale: Infiltration requires immediate stop and local assessment.

Why others are incorrect:

  • A — Unsafe continuation.
  • C — MD contact before nursing action.
  • D — Delay.

Question 7

Which learner explanation should a trainer STOP immediately?

  • A. “This option delays care.”
  • B. “This is unsafe because airway is ignored.”
  • C. “This sentence sounds more professional.”
  • D. “This exceeds nursing scope.”

Correct Answer: C

Rationale: Prometric tests behavior, not English style or professionalism cues.

Question 8

Cohort data shows 95% pass rate but high variability in rationale explanations. Trainer action?

  • A. Release cohort immediately
  • B. Ignore due to high pass rate
  • C. Audit trainer calibration and rationale language
  • D. Add more grammar lessons

Correct Answer: C

Rationale: Inconsistent rationale indicates calibration drift despite high scores.
